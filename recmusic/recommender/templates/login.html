This website is a research project in collaboration with the University of Bristol. It's main purpose is to recommend independent music artists to you depending on your music taste. I analyze your music taste by looking at any artists you mention in your tweets, as well as your friends tweets. Don't worry if you have never posted music on Twitter, you are still a valid candidate. The goal of this research is to identify if the artists recommended to you match your music taste. All you need to do is like or dislike the artist - this is located top right of each artist name. Thanks for your participation.
